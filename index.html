Distinguished Alumni Lecture Series

Distinguished Alumni Lecture Series is being coordinated by the Office of Dean Alumni Affairs and International Relations to provide a platform where distinguished alumni of NIT Calicut visit the campus and share their professional experience with the students. Distinguished alumni from academia, research laboratories and industries from India and abroad are invited to interact and motivate the students. It is envisaged to expose the students to the latest trends in the industry.

Contributions from alumini

REC/NITC alumni have been contributing to the overall growth of the institute in multifaceted ways. There have been contributions in the form of scholarships, infrastructure projects, technology projects, mentoring, lectures, joint research projects, etc. The Silver Jubilee Endowment Trust was formed with the idea of alumni contributing to award merit and means scholarships to deserving students of NIT Calicut. A total of around Ten Lakh rupees is awarded as scholarships each year to benefit around 100 students. Every batch of alumni celebrating their Silver Jubilee reunion is encouraged to make a contribution to the Silver Jubilee Endowment Fund.
